About ME

I’m Tom Thomas, a software consultant with a passion for simplifying complex technology. My expertise includes automating workflows, integrating diverse software systems, and conducting performance and basic security audits—all aimed at making your business operations more efficient, reliable, and secure.

Alongside hands-on experience with all major cloud platforms—AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP)—I also hold two key certifications:

  • CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator), awarded by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF), which certifies my ability to build and manage scalable, secure, cloud-native systems and applications.
  • Microsoft Azure Fundamentals, which validates my skills in deploying and managing solutions in Microsoft Azure, one of the most widely adopted enterprise cloud platforms.

While these certifications are technical in nature, their purpose is simple: to ensure your systems are efficient, secure, and scalable, so you can focus on growing your business without worrying about the tech behind it.

Whether it's streamlining your workflows with automation, integrating AI solutions, or ensuring your business is secure, my goal is to make technology work for you—easily and effectively.

While this is currently a side project, I treat every client engagement with the same level of care and professionalism to simplify your tech and help you move with smarter, more secure solutions.
